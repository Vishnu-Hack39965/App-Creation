----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                                To change external app name
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In AndroidManifest.xml:-

Replace "Rakshapal Library" 
(with whatever name you want to show other users.)

android:label="Rakshapal Library"

with :-
android:label="Enter your app name here"





----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                                To change internal app name
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Find this line in connectToWifi:
javascriptconst deepLink = `mylibraryapp://wifi?ssid=${encodeURIComponent(ssid)}&pass=${encodeURIComponent(pass)}`;

Replace with:
javascriptconst deepLink = `rakshapallibrary://wifissid=${encodeURIComponent(ssid)}&pass=${encodeURIComponent(pass)}`;

That's the only fix needed — one word change from mylibraryapp to rakshapallibrary.

Also fix your AndroidManifest.xml — update both intent filters to match:
xml<data android:scheme="rakshapallibrary" android:host="wifi" />
xml<data android:scheme="rakshapallibrary" android:host="forgetwifi" />

And MainActivity.kt line:
kotlinif (data.scheme != "rakshapallibrary") return

All three files must use the same scheme — rakshapallibrary everywhere.
